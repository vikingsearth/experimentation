TechVista Operations Knowledge Base: Dependencies and Flows

CustomerPortal depends on AuthGateway for login, SessionService for session refresh, and PaymentsAPI for checkout.

PaymentsAPI depends on BillingLedger to persist payment results. PaymentsAPI also depends on RiskScoring for fraud decisions before payment capture. When a payment is approved, PaymentsAPI publishes a checkout.completed event to EventBridge.

RiskScoring depends on FeatureStore for model inputs and publishes fraud.review_required events to EventBridge when a transaction must be manually reviewed.

NotificationWorker depends on EventBridge because it consumes checkout.completed and refund.completed events to notify customers.

WarehouseSync depends on EventBridge and AnalyticsIngest. It consumes checkout.completed, refund.completed, and invoice.posted events before loading curated records into Customer360.

AuthGateway depends on KeyVaultSync for OAuth signing key distribution. If KeyVaultSync delivers an expired key, CustomerPortal logins fail even when SessionService is healthy.

BillingLedger publishes invoice.posted and refund.completed events to EventBridge after settlement jobs finish.

During normal checkout, the request path is CustomerPortal to PaymentsAPI to RiskScoring to FeatureStore, then back to PaymentsAPI to BillingLedger. The post-payment event path is PaymentsAPI to EventBridge to NotificationWorker and WarehouseSync, then WarehouseSync to AnalyticsIngest to Customer360.
