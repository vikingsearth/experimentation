TechVista Operations Knowledge Base: Services and Repositories

CustomerPortal is the public web application used for login, checkout, and order history. It is maintained by the Customer Experience team. Its main repository is repo-customer-portal.

AuthGateway validates user credentials and exchanges OAuth tokens. It is owned by the Identity team. Repository: repo-auth-gateway.

SessionService manages session state and refresh tokens for CustomerPortal. It is owned by the Identity team. Repository: repo-session-service.

PaymentsAPI handles pricing, checkout authorization, and payment capture requests from CustomerPortal. It is owned by the Payments team. Repository: repo-payments-api.

BillingLedger records invoices, payment settlements, and refund adjustments. It is owned by the Payments team. Repository: repo-billing-ledger.

RiskScoring evaluates fraud and checkout risk signals for PaymentsAPI. It is co-developed by Payments and Data Platform, but operational ownership sits with the Payments team. Repository: repo-risk-scoring.

EventBridge routes internal domain events between services. It is owned by Platform Operations. Repository: repo-event-bridge.

NotificationWorker sends email and SMS updates after checkout and refund events arrive on EventBridge. It is owned by Platform Operations. Repository: repo-notification-worker.

WarehouseSync copies approved order and revenue events from EventBridge into Customer360 for analytics. It is owned by Platform Operations. Repository: repo-warehouse-sync.

FeatureStore serves model features to RiskScoring. It is owned by the Data Platform team. Repository: repo-feature-store.

AnalyticsIngest validates analytics schemas before data reaches Customer360. It is owned by the Data Platform team. Repository: repo-analytics-ingest.
