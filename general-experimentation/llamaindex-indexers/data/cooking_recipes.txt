Recipe 1: Spaghetti Carbonara
Preparation time: 10 minutes
Cooking time: 15 minutes
Serves: 4

Ingredients:
- 400g spaghetti
- 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
- 4 large egg yolks
- 2 whole eggs
- 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, finely grated
- 50g Parmesan cheese, finely grated
- Freshly ground black pepper
- Salt for pasta water

Instructions:
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook spaghetti until al dente
2. While pasta cooks, fry pancetta in a large skillet over medium heat until crispy (about 5 minutes)
3. In a bowl, whisk together egg yolks, whole eggs, Pecorino, and Parmesan
4. When pasta is ready, reserve 1 cup of pasta water, then drain
5. Add hot pasta to the skillet with pancetta (off heat)
6. Quickly pour egg mixture over pasta and toss vigorously, adding pasta water as needed
7. The residual heat will cook the eggs into a creamy sauce
8. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper

Recipe 2: Thai Green Curry
Preparation time: 15 minutes
Cooking time: 25 minutes
Serves: 4

Ingredients:
- 2 tablespoons green curry paste
- 400ml coconut milk
- 500g chicken breast, sliced
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 200g green beans, trimmed
- 100g bamboo shoots
- 2 tablespoons fish sauce
- 1 tablespoon palm sugar or brown sugar
- Fresh Thai basil leaves
- Jasmine rice for serving

Instructions:
1. Heat a large wok or pan over high heat
2. Add 3 tablespoons of the thick cream from the top of the coconut milk
3. Stir in green curry paste and cook for 1 minute until fragrant
4. Add chicken and stir-fry for 3 minutes until sealed
5. Pour in remaining coconut milk and bring to a simmer
6. Add bell pepper, green beans, and bamboo shoots
7. Season with fish sauce and sugar
8. Simmer for 15 minutes until chicken is cooked through
9. Stir in Thai basil leaves just before serving
10. Serve over steamed jasmine rice

Recipe 3: Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies
Preparation time: 15 minutes
Cooking time: 12 minutes per batch
Makes: 36 cookies

Ingredients:
- 225g butter, softened
- 150g granulated sugar
- 150g brown sugar, packed
- 2 large eggs
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 350g all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 300g chocolate chips

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 190 degrees Celsius (375 degrees Fahrenheit)
2. Cream together butter and both sugars until light and fluffy
3. Beat in eggs one at a time, then add vanilla extract
4. In a separate bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, and salt
5. Gradually mix dry ingredients into wet ingredients
6. Fold in chocolate chips
7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto lined baking sheets
8. Bake for 10-12 minutes until edges are golden but centers still soft
9. Let cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack
